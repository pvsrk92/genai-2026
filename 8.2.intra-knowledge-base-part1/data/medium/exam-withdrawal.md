# Withdrawal from exam attempts

You cannot withdraw from the 1st exam attempt without an exemption.

You can withdraw from the 2nd and 3rd exam attempt without an exemption.

You will automatically be registered for the next exam. If your registration has been withdrawn from an exam attempt at e.g. the ordinary exam, you will automatically be registered for the next exam, which is the re-take. In other words, when you withdraw, you are only withdrawing from one exam attempt at a time.

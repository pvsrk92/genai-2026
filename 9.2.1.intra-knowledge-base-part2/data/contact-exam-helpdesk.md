# Exams and timetable Helpdesk

If you have questions about your exam, your registrations for exams or classes, e.g. because:

-   you cannot see a course in Canvas
-   you cannot see an exam in Digital Exam
-   you get ill before/during an exam
-   you need to withdraw from an exam
-   you submit your exam too late

You have to contact us via [Exams Helpdesk](https://eur02.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https://helpdesk.cbs.dk/Exams&data=05%7c01%7cstudentcommunication%40cbs.dk%7cb21e410ffe4c4151218e08dbec269ff8%7c875c414e5d004cdbb77adeae5d6ab201%7c0%7c0%7c638363423388926706%7cUnknown%7cTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7c3000%7c%7c%7c&sdata=YW3Mvm/X6V/9R3HJKRIkPoWoOCmGv8Rr41%2BccWqgFso%3D&reserved=0)

You will find the exact exam dates on [Digital Exam](https://eksamen.cbs.dk/) - both for quarter and semester courses and courses at Summer University.

-   **Autumn semester**: 1 September - on [Digital Exam](https://eksamen.cbs.dk/)

-   **Summer University**: January - on [Summer University at cbs.dk](https://www.cbs.dk/en/study-programmes/summer-university/exams)

## Time and exam order for oral exams

We aim to publish the specific exam times as early as possible, so you have the opportunity to plan your semester.

#### For oral exams with a written product

Until the submission deadline, the exam schedule is tentative. This means that your exam date is fixed, but the exact time is preliminary and may change after the submission of the written product.

#### For oral exams without a written product

For oral exams without a written product, the exam schedule is generally final. However, the exact time may change in case of prior withdrawal of registration, absences, or failure to meet mandatory activity requirements. Therefore, check the exam schedule before your exam and show up in good time according to the rules.

If you are unsure about what a written product covers, you can read about [the different types of oral examinations here].

#### Exam dates are determined by CBS

You cannot request a specific date or time for your exam, nor can you swap places with another student.

More information and examples on the page [Exam plans].

#### Changes may occur - arrive ahead of time

Please note that the order for the exam is only a guideline. There is a risk of changes occurring on the day of the exam itself. This can, for instance, be due to delays or a student not showing up for the exam. This could mean that your exam will then either be earlier or later than expected. It is your responsibility to be ready at the examination room when your name is called. If you are not present, you risk using an exam attempt.

## Exam overlap?

If you have two exams scheduled at the same time, check if you are allowed extra time. However, not all exams on the same day are considered exam overlap.

Read more about exam overlaps, what counts and what to do on the page [Exam plans - Exam overlap].

## Clarify and file a complaint if you have exam overlap

If you need to clarify if you have exam overlap, first contact [a student guidance counsellor]. They can help you clarify if you have a legitimate overlap. If so, file a Helpdesk complaint via [the Exam Helpdesk](https://eur02.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https://helpdesk.cbs.dk/Exams&data=05%7c01%7cstudentcommunication%40cbs.dk%7cb21e410ffe4c4151218e08dbec269ff8%7c875c414e5d004cdbb77adeae5d6ab201%7c0%7c0%7c638363423388926706%7cUnknown%7cTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7c3000%7c%7c%7c&sdata=YW3Mvm/X6V/9R3HJKRIkPoWoOCmGv8Rr41%2BccWqgFso%3D&reserved=0).

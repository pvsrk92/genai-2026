# Illness during exams

It is difficult to perform at your best if you are ill.

If you are sick when you have an exam, then you should seriously consider withdrawing from the exam and choose to register for the retake at a later point in time.

If you cannot participate in an exam or retake due to illness, you must submit documentation for the illness no later than 8 calendar days after the date of your scheduled exam.

If you withdraw correctly, you will not use an exam attempt.

You report your sickness by following the instructions under [How to withdraw due to illness in the Exam helpdesk].

You will automatically be registered for the next exam attempt. [Read more about registration for exam attempts].

## If you fall ill at an exam

#### At the exam

-   Written sit-in exams: inform the invigilators in case of illness. The invigilators will hereafter note it down in their report.
-   Oral exams: if you suddenly fall ill while waiting for an oral exam, you do not need to wait around to notify the examinator. If you fall ill during the exam or the preparation, you must notify the examiner before you leave the exam.

#### After you have left the exam

-   Make sure that you can document the acute illness e.g. by contacting your doctor for a medical certificate. If you cannot document the acute illness, you will use an exam attempt.
-   Withdraw from the exam due to illness via the Exam helpdesk. Read more about how withdraw below
-   Participate in the exam next time it is offered

## Medical certificate requirement

You should see your doctor while you are ill, since your doctor will normally need to see you to assess your illness and issue a medical certificate. This also applies in case of acute illness.

You have to cover the expenses of the medical certificate yourself. 

The medical certificate must contain the following information:

-   Patient's name, CPR-number and address
-   Doctor's name, authorisation ID, company address and education (incl. specialist title, if applicable).
-   Date and signature of the doctor or equivalent certification.

And a description of:

-   That you are / have been ill. It does not need to state exactly which illness.
-   How your illness influences / has influenced your study activities: the doctor needs to describe how your illness has prevented / is preventing you from participating in study activities.
-   Which period you have been / are ill: and if the illness is expected to be temporary or permanent. If the illness is temporary, the dates of the period in which you are expected to be / have been ill must be stated.
-   Whether your absence is part-time or full-time


The medical certificate must be in Danish or English and be in one of the following formats: pdf, word, jpg, or tgif.

If you have a medical certificate containing all of the above, you do not need to send in medical journals or similar documentation.

## How to withdraw due to illness via the Exam helpdesk

You must withdraw due to illness no later than 8 days after the exam took place.

-   Log on to the [Exams helpdesk](https://eur02.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https://helpdesk.cbs.dk/Exams&data=05%7c01%7cstudentcommunication%40cbs.dk%7cb21e410ffe4c4151218e08dbec269ff8%7c875c414e5d004cdbb77adeae5d6ab201%7c0%7c0%7c638363423388926706%7cUnknown%7cTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7c3000%7c%7c%7c&sdata=YW3Mvm/X6V/9R3HJKRIkPoWoOCmGv8Rr41%2BccWqgFso%3D&reserved=0) using your CBS username and password
-   Choose ‘Illness at exam’ in the first drop-down menu
-   Find your course in the second drop-down menu. You can search for your course either by name or course number
-   Describe why you need to withdraw due to illness under 'Message'
-   Attach the necessary documentation under 'Attach documentation'
-   Press ‘Save’ and your case is now ready for processing - you will receive an email from the helpdesk confirming your case creation
-   You will receive an answer in the helpdesk within 7 working days
-   You will get an email from the helpdesk whenever there are updates to your case.


Please note: this does not apply for students who fall ill during their thesis. Then you should send an e-mail to [masterthesis@cbs.dk](mailto:masterthesis@cbs.dk).

#### You must upload the following documentation:

-   Medical certificate for illness

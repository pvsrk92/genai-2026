## Study boards meetings

Study Board meetings are usually public. At the meetings, the study board discusses topics such as teaching quality, evaluation and development, often in collaboration with the [Quality Board].

It is also the study board that handles e.g. complaints. The parts of the meetings that deal with matters involving personal information are confidential and reserved for study board members.

If you wish to participate in a study board meeting, please note that you do not have the right to speak at the meeting, and only members of the study board have the right to vote when decisions are made.

# Student Guidance

In Student Guidance you will meet full-time student guidance counsellors. We are all specialised in student guidance and personal guidance. We focus on your specific needs, and we have high standards for ethics and respect for the individual.

We are here to listen to your problems, both big and small, and we can be a conversation partner and sparring partner when things get critical or you need to explore your options.

We have the duty of confidentiality, and you are welcome to approach us anonymously.

## How can we help you?

You can get help and guidance with subjects such as:

-   Applying for an exemption, including how to make an alternative study plan.

-   Planning your studies, e.g., choosing electives, internship, exchange, maternity/paternity leave or programme changes.

-   Personal circumstances that affect your student life, such as stress, loneliness, work pressure, illness, or death in the family.

-   Rules that affect your studies, such as exam attempts, leave of absence, credit transfer or exam complaints.

-   Well-being during your studies, e.g., related to the academic level, problems with group work, social environment or general doubts about your programme.

-   Study technique, e.g., when reading academic texts, Danish academic culture, preparing for exams or note-taking and writing papers.

-   Problems with your thesis, thesis partner or supervisor.

-   Harassment from fellow students or employees at CBS.


## How to contact your student guidance counsellor

You are always very welcome to contact your student guidance counsellor. We offer guidance over the phone during telephone hours, via email, in our walk-in or we can book a guidance session for you if you need it.

Are you unsure about who to contact with a question about your studies, it is a good idea to start by reaching out to us. If we cannot help you with your question we will refer you to the relevant colleague, department or unit on or outside of CBS.

No question is too big or small, and most importantly, we are always happy to help you.
